We are sorry to have to inform you that you have insomnia.

Insomnia is when you aren’t sleeping as you should. That can mean you aren’t sleeping enough, you aren’t sleeping well or you’re having trouble falling or staying asleep. When insomnia is severe or lasts a long time, it causes sleep deprivation. A major concern with sleep deprivation is daytime sleepiness, which can be dangerous if you’re driving or doing other tasks that require you to be alert and attentive.

The main approaches to treating insomnia are: Developing and practicing good sleep habits, Medications that help you fall or stay asleep and Mental healthcare.
